Op_Shrt is a Bitcoin Ordinals protocol
imagined to buy t-shirts on the Bitcoin network.


The concept is simple:

Each unique ordinal inscription 'shrt's
can be redeemed to obtain a real and
unique t-shirt with the ordinal's design.

Inscriptions are first sold on the platform,
either by auction - Genesis - or by releasing
some $SHRT token.

Then you can either hold, send, or redeem
your shrt.

To get a unique t-shirt, you need to own
the shrt.

The redeem process will be detailed in
the corresponding page.
